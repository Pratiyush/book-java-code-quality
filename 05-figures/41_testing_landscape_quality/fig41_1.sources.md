**Figure:** Fig 41.1 — The test pyramid  
**Chapter:** 20 — The testing landscape & quality (dossier key 41, Part V opener)  
**One idea:** The pyramid encodes a cost/speed/confidence trade-off — many fast unit tests at the base, fewer integration tests in the middle, fewest E2E tests at the apex — and its inversion (the ice-cream cone) is the named failure mode to prevent.
