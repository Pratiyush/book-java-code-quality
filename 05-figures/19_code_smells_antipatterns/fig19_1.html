A — Metric-threshold
A counter against a configurable limit. The threshold is a convention, not a law — tools disagree on the number, which is the lesson. Gate-able in CI at a tuned threshold.
B — Structural-pattern
An AST shape match with no number. Some have a rule (PMD LawOfDemeter); others (Feature Envy, Speculative Generality) have no reliable detector. Review-found, not tool-found.
C — Contract / bug-overlap
Bytecode or flow analysis that overlaps with genuine defects. These are not just stylistic: the EI_EXPOSE_REP smell is also a real latent mutation bug. Hard gate in CI.
The smell is a hint to investigate, never a verdict to obey. A 60-line method may be clearer whole than split (the APoSD counter-point — Ousterhout). A rule fires when a threshold is crossed, not when a refactoring is warranted. The detection mode tells you who finds the smell; the triple tells you what to do when they do.
