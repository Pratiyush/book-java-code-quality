Lineage: W. Edwards Deming — build quality into the process rather than inspecting it at the end (manufacturing quality). Larry Smith — "shift-left testing": move testing and quality activities toward inception, shorten the feedback loop (Dr. Dobb's, 2001). In this book's terms, shift-left is the lifecycle map from Chapter 3: each layer catches problems earlier and cheaper than the next. Culture decides whether developers welcome that feedback or route around it.
