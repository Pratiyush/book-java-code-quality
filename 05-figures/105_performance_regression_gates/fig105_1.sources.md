- One idea: A performance-regression gate measures a change against a baseline and acts on the result, but because the measurement is *noisy* it compares relative-to-baseline with statistics and a tolerance band, and *flags-then-investigates* a small diff rather than hard-blocking — failing safe when it cannot be sure.
