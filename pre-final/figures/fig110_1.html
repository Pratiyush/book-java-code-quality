Start where YOUR pain is — the order is a default, not a track. A team with a security mandate starts with the security gate; a team drowning in incidents jumps to Stage 3 observability first; a team with a specific hotspot remediates that before anything else. Climb for outcomes (fewer incidents, safer delivery, a changeable codebase), not for the level. Stage 0 is marked as the default "Monday" entry — it is not obligatory if a higher stage solves your most expensive problem first. There is no "done."
