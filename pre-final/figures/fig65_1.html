SLSA v1.0 — build integrity levels
L1 — provenance exists (signed)
L2 — hosted build service; non-falsifiable
L3 — hardened, hermetic build platform
A roadmap, not a single tool. Higher levels = real investment. Most teams start at L1.
⚠ SCA: known vulns only

Zero-days and first-party bugs are out of scope. A clean scan is not a clean bill of health. That gap is SAST + secure coding (Part VIII).

⚠ Vulnerable ≠ exploitable

CPE matching is fuzzy — flags CVEs in paths your app never calls. Reachability analysis (some commercial tiers) narrows this. Suppression + justification required (Ch 19).

⚠ SBOM: inventory, not defense

Enables a fast response; fixes nothing. Generated and ignored = theatre. Accuracy gaps: misses shaded, dynamically-loaded, or vendored components.

⚠ Compliance driver

US EO 14028 and EU Cyber Resilience Act make SBOMs mandatory in many sectors. Supply-chain hygiene is becoming a legal obligation, not only best practice.
