Principle: minimize mutability — stands regardless of which form is chosen above (EJ Items 15–17)
Folklore to avoid: "Records replace the immutability item." They do not. A record exposes all components and is for transparent data. A class with behaviour, encapsulated state, or validation beyond a compact constructor is not a record candidate. Use the feature where it fits the principle — not as a default.
