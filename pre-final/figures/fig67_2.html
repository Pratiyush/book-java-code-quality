Not legal advice
Tools detect the declared license; they do not interpret your specific obligations. The categories here are factual; license strategy and the policy itself are questions for legal counsel, not a build plugin.
Detection is imperfect
Missing, ambiguous, multi-licensed, relicensed, and dual-licensed components confuse scanners; a POM-declared license may not be the actual one. Verify high-risk findings by hand.
Transitive surprise · license ≠ security
A permissive direct dependency can pull a copyleft transitive — scan the full graph (Ch 27). And a license-clean dependency can still be vulnerable (Ch 28).
